    Section 2 Course Information

    Subsection 2.1 Course Description

    Vectors, three-dimensional coordinates, double and triple integrals, vector valued functions and motion in space.

    Subsection 2.2 Course Prerequisites

    A grade of C or better in MATH 242

    Subsection 2.3 Student Learning Outcomes

    The student will become proficient in vectors and the geometry of space, vector functions, and multiple integration. This proficiency will be demonstrated by satisfactorily completing a series of exams and homework assignments.

    Subsection 2.4 Course Topics

    Topics include
    • Vectors and the Geomtry of Space
    • Planes and Surfaces
    • Parametrization
    • Functions of Several Real Variables
    • Multiple Integration
    • Vector-Valued Functions

    Subsection 2.5 Instructional Methods

    This course is offered as a face-to-face course. Learning will be facilitated through traditional lecture, group work/activities, homework, and in-class assessments.
